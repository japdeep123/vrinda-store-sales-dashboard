# 🛍️ Vrinda Store Annual Sales Dashboard 2022

**Tools:** Excel · PivotTables · Power Query · Data Visualisation | **Type:** Guided Project | **Instructor:** Rishabh Mishra

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyses **Vrinda Store's 2022 annual sales data** to uncover revenue trends, top-performing channels, customer demographics, and regional performance — built as an interactive Excel dashboard to support **data-driven business decisions**.

> **Key Finding:** Women account for **64% of total purchases**, and **Amazon, Flipkart & Myntra** together drive **80% of total orders** — indicating where marketing spend should be concentrated.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- Which months generated the highest sales and orders?
- Who buys more — men or women?
- Which sales channels drive the most revenue?
- Which states are the top contributors to sales?
- What is the order status breakdown (Delivered, Returned, Cancelled)?
